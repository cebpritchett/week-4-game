Get the Chaos Emeralds!

You will be given a randum number at the start of the game.



There are four Chaos Emeralds below. By clicking on an emerald you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.



You win the game by matching your total score to a random number, you lose the game if your total score goes over the random number.



The value of each emerald is hidden from you until you click on it.



Each time the game is started, the value of the emeralds will change.
